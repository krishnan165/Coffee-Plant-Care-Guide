Coffee Plant Care Guide

Soil for Coffee Plants

Well-drained, acidic soil.

Coffee plants thrive in well-drained and acidic soil. Ensure good drainage for optimal root health.

Watering

Regular and moderate watering.

Water coffee plants regularly, keeping the soil consistently moist but not waterlogged.

Sunlight

Filtered sunlight.

Provide filtered sunlight to coffee plants, avoiding direct, intense sunlight for extended periods.

Benefits

Homegrown coffee beans.

Growing coffee plants at home allows you to harvest your own coffee beans for a unique and fresh brew.

Greenery and aesthetics.

Coffee plants add a touch of greenery to your space and contribute to a visually pleasing environment.

Considerations

Protect from cold drafts; watch for pests such as aphids and scale insects.
